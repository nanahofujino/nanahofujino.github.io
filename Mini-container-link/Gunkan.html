What is Gunkanmaki?

Gunkanmaki is a sushi which looks like warship. It is made by the rice rapped by Nori and raw fish or fish egg.

Type of Gunkanmaki

  • Uni 海栗
  • Ikura いくら
  • Tobiko トビコ
  • Negitoro ねぎとろ
  • Shirauo 白魚
  • Okura オクラ
